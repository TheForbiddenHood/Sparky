# Privacy Policy: Sparky

## 1. Information We Collect
### Discord User Data
We collect the user's Discord User ID, Username, and Avatar URL from the Discord API.
### Inputted Data
Data provided via "/register"
### Check-in Data
When using a "Sparky Swipe" station, we collect the Timestamp of the swipe, the Station ID, and the Event ID associated with your check-in.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
To verify your attendance at physical events and provide check-in confirmation back to your Discord account.

## 3. Data Sharing & Third Parties
### Event Organizers
When you use a "Sparky Swipe" station, your Discord identity (Username/ID) is shared with the specific event organizer for that station at that time. However, we do not share your full command history, or private data with event organizers - only the data that is collected through alternative means such as a QR code.
### Selling of Data
Outside of these authorized partnered events, we do not sell, trade, or barter your data to any third parties.

## 4. Data Security
Data sent from a "Sparky Swipe" station to our data base on campus is encrypted to prevent interception during the check-in process.

## 5. Your Rights
You may stop using the "Sparky Swipe" stations at any time. To have previous check-in logs deleted, please contact @emilioondisc on Discord.
